# Adaire Blocks v1.1.7 Release Notes

## New Animation on Scroll Block - Bring Your Content to Life!

We're excited to introduce a powerful new block that lets you create stunning scroll-triggered animations with complete control over every detail.

### What's New

#### 🎬 Animation on Scroll Block (Brand New!)
Create eye-catching animations that trigger as users scroll down your page. Choose from 12 different animation types including fades, fly-ins, rotations, flips, and more!

**Key Features:**
- **12 Animation Types**: Fade in/out, fly up/down/left/right, grow, shrink, bounce, flip, rotate, blur effects
- **Live Preview**: See your animations in real-time as you edit in the block editor
- **Complete Control**: Adjust animation speed, delay, distance, and easing
- **Flip Options**: Control whether flips are horizontal or vertical, clockwise or anti-clockwise
- **Smart Reversal**: Animations reverse smoothly when scrolling back up or down
- **Responsive Design**: Different settings for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **One-Time Animations**: Option to animate only on first view

### Why You'll Love This

✨ **Professional Animations**: Create smooth, polished animations that enhance your site's visual appeal

🎯 **Easy to Use**: Intuitive controls with live preview - no coding required

⚡ **Performance Optimized**: Hardware-accelerated animations that don't slow down your site

📱 **Responsive**: Looks great on all devices with separate controls for different screen sizes

🔄 **Flexible**: Trigger animations when content enters view, with customizable threshold and delay

### How to Use It

1. Add the "Animation on Scroll" block to your page
2. Choose your animation type (fade, fly, rotate, flip, etc.)
3. Adjust the settings to your liking
4. Add any content inside the block (text, images, buttons, etc.)
5. Watch it come to life as users scroll!

### Perfect For

- **Hero Sections**: Make your hero content pop with smooth entrances
- **Featured Content**: Draw attention to important sections
- **Call-to-Actions**: Add eye-catching animations to CTAs
- **Testimonials**: Make reviews and testimonials stand out
- **Portfolio Items**: Showcase your work with dynamic presentations
- **Any Content**: Add motion to any block or content area

### What's Improved

- **Better Flip Animations**: Optimized flip effects that don't overflow the page
- **Smarter Thresholds**: Improved handling when using delay and threshold together
- **Bidirectional Reversals**: Animations reverse smoothly when scrolling in either direction
- **Live Editor Preview**: See exactly how animations will look before publishing

### Getting Started

The Animation on Scroll block is available in the block inserter under the "Widgets" category. Simply add it to your page, choose your settings, add your content, and you're ready to go!

**Update Notes**: This is a feature update with no breaking changes. All your existing content will continue to work exactly as before.
