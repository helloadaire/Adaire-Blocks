# Adaire Blocks v1.2.0 – Five New Blocks & Enhanced Management

This update brings five powerful new blocks plus significant improvements to how you manage and organize your blocks in the WordPress admin.

## New Blocks

### Flip Card Block (Plus)
Create interactive flip cards that reveal content on hover or click. Add any blocks to the front and back sides, customize the flip animation, and control every aspect of the card's appearance. Perfect for showcasing services, team members, or product features with engaging 3D transitions.

### Pricing Table Block (Plus)
Build professional pricing tables with monthly/yearly billing toggles. Customize each pricing card with features, buttons, colors, and styling. Set different column layouts for desktop, tablet, and mobile, and highlight featured plans with distinct styling. Complete typography and spacing controls let you match your brand perfectly.

### Progress Block (Plus)
Display scroll-based progress indicators that fill as visitors scroll down your page. Choose between a horizontal bar or circular ring design, position it anywhere on the screen, and customize colors, sizing, and appearance. Great for showing reading progress on long-form content.

### Animation on Scroll Block (Free)
Add smooth scroll-triggered animations to any content. Choose from 12 animation types including fade, slide, zoom, rotate, and bounce effects. Configure animation distance, duration, and timing to create engaging entrance effects that bring your content to life as users scroll.

### Social Share Block (Free)
Add social sharing buttons to your pages with a clean, customizable interface. Select which platforms to show, customize the button appearance, and let visitors share your content with one click. Supports Facebook, X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Telegram, Reddit, Pinterest, and Email.

### Testimonial 3 Block (Premium)
A premium testimonial carousel with a distinctive purple background and white card design. Features draggable navigation, profile images, and unlimited testimonials. Perfect for showcasing client feedback with a polished, professional appearance.

## Improved Block Management

### Organized by Tier
Blocks are now organized by tier (Free, Plus, Premium) in the admin settings page, making it easier to see what's available at each level and manage your block library.

### Cleaner Interface
Auxiliary blocks like Content Toggle Panel and Swiper Slide are now hidden from the main interface but still work automatically with their parent blocks. This reduces clutter while maintaining full functionality.

### Consistent Icons
All blocks now display consistent, polished icons throughout the editor, block management page, and documentation for a more professional experience.

## Getting Started

All new blocks are available immediately in the block inserter. Search for "Flip Card", "Pricing Table", "Progress", "Animation on Scroll", "Social Share", or "Testimonial 3" to get started.

The Animation on Scroll and Social Share blocks are available in the free version, while Flip Card, Pricing Table, and Progress require Plus or Premium. Testimonial 3 is exclusive to Premium.

## Why Upgrade?

- More interactive elements: flip cards, pricing tables, and progress indicators
- Better content engagement with scroll animations
- Easier social sharing integration

No migrations or breaking changes—just update and start using the new blocks.
