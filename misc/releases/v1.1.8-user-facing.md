# Adaire Blocks v1.1.8 Release Notes

## New Blocks - Modal Block & Industries Block!

We're excited to introduce two powerful new blocks: the **Modal Block** for creating customizable popup dialogs, and the **Industries Block** for showcasing industries with beautiful responsive layouts, icons, and flexible content management.

### What's New

#### 🎯 Modal Block (Brand New!)
Create professional popup modals with trigger buttons and flexible content areas. Perfect for forms, announcements, image galleries, and more!

#### 🏢 Industries Block (Brand New!)
Showcase your industries with beautiful responsive tiles, customizable icons, and flexible layouts. Perfect for service pages, industry showcases, and business highlights!

**Key Features:**
- **Flexible Content**: Add any Gutenberg blocks to both trigger and modal content
- **Responsive Dimensions**: Separate width and height controls for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Complete Customization**: Control colors, borders, padding, and close button appearance
- **Live Preview**: See your modal appearance in real-time as you edit
- **Accessibility Ready**: Full keyboard navigation, focus management, and screen reader support
- **Smooth Animations**: Professional opening and closing animations
- **Unobtrusive**: Inline display minimizes visual obstruction in the editor

- **Unlimited Industries**: Add as many industries as you need - layout adapts automatically
- **Customizable Styling**: Full control over colors, typography, spacing, and hover effects

### How to Use It

**Modal Block:**
1. Add the "Modal" block to your page
2. Customize the trigger block (default: button) or add your own content
3. Add your modal content (default: heading + paragraph) or add any blocks you want
4. Set dimensions for desktop, tablet, and mobile
5. Customize colors, borders, padding, and close button
6. Use the preview toggle to see how it looks
7. Publish and click the trigger to open your modal!

**Industries Block:**
1. Add the "Industries" block to your page
2. Edit the headlines and intro text to match your brand
3. Click "Add Industry" to add new industries
4. Use the icon picker to choose icons for each industry
5. Optionally add links to make industries clickable
6. Use move up/down buttons to reorder industries
7. Toggle visibility of title, intro text, or image as needed
8. Optionally hide first two industries if you want to start from the third
9. Customize colors, fonts, and styling to match your design
10. Publish and see your beautiful industry showcase!

### Getting Started

Both the Modal Block and Industries Block are available in the block inserter under the "Widgets" category. 

**For Modal Block**: Simply add it to your page, customize the trigger and content, set your dimensions and styling, and you're ready to go!

**For Industries Block**: Add it to your page, customize the headlines, add your industries with icons, set up links if needed, and customize the styling to match your brand!

### Tips for Best Results

**Modal Block:**
- **Plan Your Content**: Think about what you want in the modal before setting dimensions
- **Use Responsive Units**: Use percentage units for responsive designs that work across screen sizes
- **Test on Mobile**: Always test your modals on mobile devices for touch interactions
- **Keep Content Light**: Keep modal content focused and lightweight for best performance
- **Accessibility**: Include descriptive text in trigger buttons for screen readers

**Industries Block:**
- **Choose Meaningful Icons**: Select icons that represent each industry clearly
- **Use Descriptive Labels**: Make industry labels clear and concise
- **Add Links**: Make industries clickable to link to relevant pages or sections
- **Test Visibility**: Use visibility toggles to see how layout changes affect your design
- **Mobile Testing**: Always preview on mobile to see how the responsive grid looks
- **Consistent Styling**: Use consistent colors and fonts to maintain brand identity
